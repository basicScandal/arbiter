How the AI Judge Scores

Every demo is evaluated across three core dimensions, weighted and blended with human judge scores for the final result.

The Three Pillars

Technical Execution 40%

Does it work? How well is it built? The AI evaluates implementation quality, code correctness, complexity, and how gracefully edge cases are handled.

What Earns Top Marks
Flawless, production-quality implementation that handles edge cases with elegance.
Innovation 30%

Is this something new? The AI looks for novel approaches at the intersection of AI and security — creative thinking that pushes boundaries.

What Earns Top Marks
A groundbreaking, never-before-seen approach that redefines what's possible.
Demo Quality 30%

Can you show it and explain it? The AI judges the clarity of the live demo, the strength of the explanation, and how compelling the narrative is.

What Earns Top Marks
A flawless live demo with masterful explanation and a compelling story.
