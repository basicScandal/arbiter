## Notes

- A team can win both a placement award AND a track award
- Track awards go to the highest-scoring team in that track (by Arbiter + human blended score)
- If the track winner is also a top-3 placer, the track award goes to the next-highest team in that track
- Audience Favorite is determined by live vote at the end of the event
- Best Injection Attempt is judged by Arbiter's roast quality + creativity of the attempt
