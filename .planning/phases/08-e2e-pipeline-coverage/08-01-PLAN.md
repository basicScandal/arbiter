**Test: `test_full_pipeline_chain`**

Drive the chain by simulating the demo lifecycle:
1. Publish `DemoStarted(team_name="TestTeam")` -- this resets defense state and sets `_current_team`.
2. `await asyncio.sleep(0)` to let handlers run.
3. Publish `DemoStopped(team_name="TestTeam", duration=180.0)` -- this triggers defense `_on_demo_stopped` which sanitizes observations and publishes `ObservationVerified`.
4. Use `await event_collector.wait_for("observation_verified", timeout=5.0)` to confirm defense published.
5. Use `await event_collector.wait_for("commentary_delivered", timeout=5.0)` to confirm commentary completed.
6. Use `await event_collector.wait_for("scoring_complete", timeout=5.0)` to confirm scoring completed.
7. Assert causal event ordering: `observation_verified` index < `scoring_complete` index AND `observation_verified` index < `commentary_delivered` index in `event_collector.events`.
8. Assert that `deliberation._memory_store.save` was called (memory auto-saved on `observation_verified`).
9. Do NOT assert ordering between `scoring_complete` and `commentary_delivered` -- they are parallel subscribers with non-deterministic ordering.

**Test: `test_full_chain_publishes_score_revealed_after_reveal`**

After the chain above completes, verify the score reveal fires:
